What are green roofs?


Green roofs are vegetated systems planted over a waterproofing layer on roofs.

They increase the vegetated area in urban landscapes and provide multiple environmental, social, and economic benefits, including:

  • Aesthetically pleasant landscapes
  • Surface rainwater runoff reduction
  • Reduction of energy consumption
  • Noise and air quality improvement
  • Urban heat island mitigation
  • Biodiversity enhancement
