Maintenance visits can be carried out with the following frequency:


2 maintenance visits per year (ogni 6 mesi)

44 maintenance visits per year (ogni 3 mesi)

6 maintenance visits per year (ogni 2 mesi)


The guidelines of the German Landscape Research, Development and Construction Society (FLL) suggest that the frequency of maintenance visits should be between 2 and 4 visits per year. Six maintenance visits could improve the healthy status of the green roof though.
