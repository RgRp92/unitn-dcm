Frequency of maintenance visits

Maintenance visits should be carried out to sustain the greening and ecological objectives of the installations. Maintenance visits include preventing and corrective tasks , such as:

  • Maintain ecological quality: preserve flora and fauna habitats.
    • Supply of nutrients and fertilizing
    • Seeding and replanting (if needed)
    • Refilling the substrate
  • Maintain aesthetics and ornamental qualities
    • Removal of unwanted growth
    • Pruning and trimming
