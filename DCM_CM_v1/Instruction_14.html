Green roofs can absorb between the 20% and 90% of rainwater depending on the green roof’s design (substrate material, depth, type of vegetation, slope) and rainfall intensity rates.

In this study, we ask you to consider three possible levels of the average rainwater absorption rate that can be attained by three different planning projects elaborated for the municipality of Trento:

30% of rainwater absorbed per square meter

40% of rainwater absorbed per square meter

50% of rainwater absorbed per square meter

Different absorptions rates depend on the green roof’s design (substrate material, depth, type of vegetation, slope) and rainfall intensity rates.
